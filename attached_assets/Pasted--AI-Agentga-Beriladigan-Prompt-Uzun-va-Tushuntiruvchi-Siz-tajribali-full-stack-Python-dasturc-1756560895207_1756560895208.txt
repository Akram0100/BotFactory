📌 Ish Ketma-ketligi (Qadamlar)

SQLAlchemy modellarini yarating: User, Bot, Subscription.
API endpointlar
Foydalanuvchilar: /api/users — ro'yxatdan o'tish, login
Botlar: /api/bots — yaratish, yangilash, o'chirish
Obuna: /api/subscriptions — bepul sinov, obuna turini tanlash
AI xizmati

Telegram bot
services/telegram_service.py — Telegramda foydalanuvchi xabarini qabul qilish va AI javobini yuborish
Frontend (ajralgan repl sifatida)
Yangi Next.js repl yarating.
Foydalanuvchi dashboardi: login, bot yaratish, sozlamalar
Testlash
